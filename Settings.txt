

Source code on off-axis hologram generation and reconstruction, together with 2 sample images.

Sample images:

ctu.bmp, pep.bmp

Hologram type:

Off-axis Fresnel hologram

Double depth: Left side of image: 0.35m, right side of image 0.38m from hologram, respectively

Optical settings:

Wavelength: 650nm

Pixel size: 7um

Hologram/image size: 2048x2048

Reference beam: Plane wave

Angle of incidence of reference beam: 0.8 degree along vertical direction

Reconstruction distance:

0.35m
